WHAT MAKES CEC SPECIAL?

Despite our passionate instructors and curated NIME curriculum, CEC employs five main types
of teaching styles and methods depending on the interest, need, strength and weakness of the
students. By employing The Authority method, The Demonstrator method, The Facilitator style,
The Delegator style and the Hybrid method, our team devises the suitable teaching style for our
students (and their admission applications) that will enable each and every one of them to
blossom in their fullness. 

 
 

VISION/OBJECTIVE/MISSION
