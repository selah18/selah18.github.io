STORY

Sookoo Coffee is a Family Owned Company that Produces and Exports Organic, High-Quality, Sustainable and Traceable Specialty Coffee from the High Altitudes Semi-Forest of Guji, located in the South of Oromia Mountains - Ethiopia!

Guji is Home to the Top National and International Award Winning Ethiopian Specialty Coffee!

“Lack no coffee nor peace, in your house”
~ Ethiopian Proverb ~

OUR PHILOSOPHY

We pay attention to details focusing on high quality, traceable and sustainable coffee.

QUALITY

Our coffee quality is due to selecting the right variety to its perfect micro-climates, and harvesting with care and processing professionally.

TRACEABILITY

Traceability is a means to track quality, every activity, and the history of our lots. Generally, all about Transparency.

SUSTAINABILITY

We believe we will sustain only if we can sustain all involved people and the environment. Our coffee is cultivated in a way that is friendly to the environment and people!

OUR PROCCESS

PLANTING

The process of coffee from it's seedling to it's fruit production requires a few years of work and patience. Thats why we get directly involved in the early period of plantation.

HARVESTING

We carefully focus while harvesting and work with small groups and provide professional training to harvest cherries when they are fully ripe. Since it takes a full year to ripen after flowering.

PROCCESING

Our unique natural proccessing is what we think separates our coffees from the rest of the world. Which includes:

Natural Proccesing ➟ Rest ➟ Hand Sorting ➟ Color Sorting

EXPORTING

After thorough proccessing our coffee is packed either in Jute Bag or Cartoon Box inside ecotact or GrainPro bag zipper to prevent it from odour or moisture.

SUSTAINABILITY IN ACTION
