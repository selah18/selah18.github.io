WHO WE ARE

Adi Coffee exporter is sole proprietorship company, owned by Mr Bersisa Ensene established according to the commercial code of Ethiopia and licensed from the Ministry of Trade. It has also secured certificate of competence for coffee export qualification issued by Ethiopian Coffee and Tea Authority.

The Name "Adi" Is In Afaan Oromo Literally Which Means White Colour In English,
Which Is A Symbol Of Trustworthy, Pure, Transparent And Bright Light
And An Expression Of Cleanness.

OUR PRINCIPLE

CLEAN

We are committed to produce, process and delivery very high-quality Coffee product & service and prioritizing environmentally issues and working on ecosystem friendly bases.

DEDICATE

We are devoted to relationship with our customers and suppliers as equal, longterm Business partnership by seeking mutual benefit.

FAIR

We Offer fair and accessible prices for clients and paying reasonable price for our farmers, out growers and suppliers, on win-win bases.

  • VISION
  • MISSION
  • OBJECTIVE

To be reliable and responsible for its customers identified by its fair price, very high quality, and timely shipment with maximum devotion and to be one of the leading coffee exporters in the country in 2030.

To deliver premium quality Ethiopian green coffee bean to the Mainstream and specialty coffee market and to build a relationship that will end up in coffee roasting and instant coffee plants.

To engage in export of premium quality coffee and win international market from its traceable specialty coffee. To promote the Ethiopian coffee to the world market in better ways than ever, to deliver value added coffees to the world market and to increase its export volume by many folds to the international market. To Working closely with coffee farmers, out growers and local suppliers in order to secure the best and quality coffees in our operation areas by providing continuous trainings to secure mutual benefits. To Create strong network among stakeholders for the sustainability of coffee and the environment.
