STORY

When Prateek was six years old, his father would make monthly visits to the local homeless shelter to give hands to those who are in need. The experience of seeing this as a kid inspired Prateek to continue what his father started and later to begin his own organization.

Prateek's father always say “सेवा ही लक्ष्य” and thus Prateek founded "Rajkumar Sharma Foundation" to continiue the legacy his father has established.

“ सेवा ही लक्ष्य ”

OUR PRINCIPLE

We pay attention to make change in peoples life by providing quality education, health care and sustaianable environment :

QUALITY EDUCATION

Enhance educational opportunities for village students of the community by providing consultancy, guiding books and tutors.

HEALTH CARE

Providing health care and improve access to health care interstate and intercontnient.

SUSTAINABILITY

We believe we will sustain only if we can sustain all involved people and the environment. We believe that farms should be cultivated in a way that is friendly to the environment and people!
